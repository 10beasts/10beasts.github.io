Best Gaming Laptops 2017 – Top 10 Notebooks for Gamers Reviewed

by Edward Eugen | Last Updated January 1, 2017

Making a list of best gaming laptops 2017 is a tricky task for sure. The cost vs. quality argument is the first thing you deal with. Nevertheless, the specs should be your top priority, ideally.

GraphicsRAM & ProcessorPower EfficiencyDisplayStorageAudioBattery

The graphics processor is what matters in gaming laptops. It is the component, which determines whether the game loads at 6 or 60 frames per second. A gaming laptop is all about the fast loading graphics.

It’s safe to say that only a great graphics processor would justify the billing.

A few more components must be present to compliment even the mightiest graphics processor.

NVidia 900 series and to an extent, the 800 series has managed to create a similar impact in the graphic processor market.

Speaking of supporting components, main processor and system RAM are your immediate priorities.

Irrespective of other specs, these two factors alone determines the range of your laptop. The benchmark for RAM and processor has rocketed, in regards to best laptops for gaming.

The choice of processors is often won hands down by either Intel or NVidia and rightly so. They perform a lot smoother and efficient than the standard alternative, AMD. The sixth generation Intel processor has upped the range in regards to power efficiency.

The power efficiency would translate to the ultimate gaming experience. Another desirable benefit would be the easy maintenance of your device.

Power efficient processors don’t let your laptop get excessively heated. The cooling effect is a must-have factor in gaming laptops. The best laptops for gamers 2017 is expected to tick this critical box.

When you have games with gameplay over a dozen hours, a heated laptop is the last thing you would wish for. It should come as an unwanted disturbance, making your gaming experience terrible. The heating effect is the sole reason, AMD processors are preferred a lot less than Intel.

Another important component which is often overlooked when making your choice amongst the best gaming laptops 2017 is the display.

What matters is what you see in regards to quality of modern age games. In retro-gaming, it was all about finding a pattern. But games of this millennium are all about getting a feel of it as quickly as possible. Resolutions like Full HD or 1920x1080p alone can exactly deliver what you need.

4K is a far superior resolution, but opting for it would be a little too early for now. Nonetheless, don’t bother too much about brightness- contrast ratios. Practical anti-glare finishes have become mandatory for today’s gaming laptops.

Storage cannot be overlooked while assessing best laptops for gamers. It won’t speed up your games for sure. But it can prove handy in reducing the delay between each level.

It serves on practicality by helping to speed up boot and file operations. SSD is the buzzword for the last couple of years. You can’t be content with anything less than that as a hardcore gamer.

An additional high capacity disk within your laptop wouldn’t hurt too. You can keep the additional solid drive for saving games.

After visuals, audio is the first thing you must focus on.

Though using headsets would feel lovely for gaming, your gaming laptop must bag excellent built-in stereo speakers also. Most modern gaming laptops operate very quietly. There won’t be much noise to cause disturbance during intense gaming.

When paired with a gaming keyboard, chances of annoying keystrokes are also eliminated. Some bespoke audio systems have made their mark to laptop speakers in the last 2-3 years. Such brands include Harman, Onkyo, B&O and more. You may choose to up the ante by setting up home theater or sound surround.

Last but not least, battery life shouldn’t be counted out, when choosing your gaming laptop.

The toughest challenge any manufacturer has is to present decent battery life. It’s not every day you go looking for a desktop replacement gaming laptop. For gaming, you need a long run off battery in every cycle. However combining fast graphic and superior battery life is one really hard challenge.

Amongst the best gaming laptops 2017, you might have to compromise on battery life more often than not. It is frequently compromised for the sake of superior configuration and key specifications.

The gaming laptops also come with few hiccups and limitations, which you must be aware of. So, let’s have a look at few of the limitations.

  • First and foremost, the Heat issues. Professional Gaming laptops accomplish each and every task with full potential which results in the great amount of heat.
  • Battery life is also a headache when it comes to gaming machines. Most of the manufacturers are messing with battery life and still there is no any laptop with a great battery backup. The average battery backup from a gaming laptop is 4-5 hours maximum.
  • Weight! Ohh, trust me weight sucks! They are so heavy and aren’t that much portable. The average weight is 7-8 pounds.
  • Gaming laptops are very expensive. You must have a good amount of money if you are on a hunt for the best laptop for gaming.
